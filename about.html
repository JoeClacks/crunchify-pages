About Crunchify.net

Crunchify.net is a collection of 39 free online tools — calculators, converters, encoders, and more. Every tool runs entirely in your browser with no server calls, no ads, and no tracking of any kind.

Most online tool sites are bloated with ads, pop-ups, and trackers. Crunchify.net is the opposite: fast, clean, and private. Your data never leaves your device.

How it works

  • 100% client-side — all computation happens in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
  • No accounts — nothing to sign up for. Just open a tool and use it.
  • No ads or tracking — no analytics, no cookies, no fingerprinting.
  • Works offline — once loaded, tools work without an internet connection.
